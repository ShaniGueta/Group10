Healthy teeth employs professional, experienced and skilled dentists, as well as experts in the following treatment areas: root canals, fillings, tartar removal, teeth whitening and extractions.
We place emphasis on the professionalism of the doctors and make sure that they undergo further training and are updated with innovations in the field of dentistry.
The clinic is equipped with equipment and technological instruments from the leaders in the field and dental materials from the best and most advanced of their kind. The clinic is under strict supervision of the Ministry of Health.
